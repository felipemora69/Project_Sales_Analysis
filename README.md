#Retail Sales Analysis & Business Insights

A Python project for uncovering trends, performance drivers, and commercial opportunities in retail operations.

**Project Overview**

This project delivers a comprehensive analysis of retail sales data using Python. It uncovers key trends, identifies top‑performing products, evaluates customer behavior, and highlights growth opportunities across channels and time periods.
The goal is to support data‑driven commercial decision‑making through clear insights and actionable analytics.

**Business Objectives**

- Understand overall sales performance and revenue trends
- Identify top‑performing products and underperforming segments
- Analyze customer purchasing behavior and loyalty patterns
- Evaluate channel effectiveness and promotional impact
- Detect seasonality and time‑based performance shifts
- Support strategic decisions in sales, inventory, and marketing

**Key Insights Delivered**
- Month‑over‑month and year‑over‑year sales trends
- Product performance ranking and Pareto 80/20 distribution
- Customer segmentation and repeat‑purchase behavior
- Channel contribution analysis
- Promotion vs. non‑promotion performance
- Revenue drivers and conversion patterns

**Technologies & Libraries**

- Python
- pandas — data cleaning & transformation
- numpy — numerical operations
- matplotlib — data visualization
- Jupyter Notebook — analysis environment

**Data Preparation**

Key steps performed during preprocessing:
- Handling missing values
- Standardizing product and customer fields
- Creating time‑based features (month, year, season)
- Calculating KPIs (AOV, revenue, units sold, retention)
- Merging datasets for unified analysis

**Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)**
The analysis includes:
- Sales trends over time
- Product category performance
- Customer purchase frequency
- Channel contribution and profitability
- Promotion vs. non‑promotion comparison
- Outlier detection and distribution analysis

**Results & Business Impact**

This analysis helps retail teams:
- Optimize inventory based on demand patterns
- Improve targeting and promotional strategies
- Identify high‑value customer segments
- Strengthen sales forecasting
- Focus on products and channels that drive the most revenue
